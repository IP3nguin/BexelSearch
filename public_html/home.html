Welcome To Bexel

The only tool you need to find and host classes in your area.

Bexel is a app designed to allow you to quickly and easily find classes in your area for whatever skill or hobby you are interested. What ever you are looking for you can search for it and see locations near you where that class is going on.

More of a teacher?

Bexel is community driven and the tutors you find are user just like you.

If you are interested in running a class of you own, just use our easy interface to create your own class listing and how it included on our map!
